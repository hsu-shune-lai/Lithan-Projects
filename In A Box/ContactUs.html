About Us

Our Company

In the summer of 2012, our founders tested the first "In a Box" recipes in their tiny New York City apartments with the goal of making the experience of cooking with quality produce and specialty ingredients accessible to everyone—no matter where they live or how busy they are. Today, we send over four million meals per month to home cooks nationwide. Instrumental to this success are our fulfillment center employees who ensure that each "In a box" box is packed to perfection. At our two FDA-regulated fulfillment centers, our employees’ health and safety is a top priority. In addition to implementing strict safety standards, we know that the COVID-19 pandemic affects every part of a person’s life. We offer numerous schedule options to help you take care of the people who depend on you. Additionally, full-time "In a Box" employees are eligible for competitive health benefits, a 401K, paid time off, and discounts on our meals. Our fulfillment centers also host weekly farmers markets where employees can take home fresh produce, protein, and spices for free!



Our Team



The CX Team Lead position requires a highly motivated and enthusiastic leader who loves to work with people. Team Leaders support all CX Associates and motivate them to do their job efficiently by monitoring and reporting performance. In addition, providing any additional cross-functional support if needed. They provide daily objectives, develop reward systems for productivity that motivate new hires and seasoned workers alike, and communicate any issues with management to reach business goals effectively. The CX Team Lead will report to the CX Manager

Executive Assistant

The Executive Assistant supports 3 Executives (Chief People Officer, Chief Finance Officer, Chief Revenue Officer) at In a Box with primarily administrative tasks related to the Executives responsibilities at In a Box. He/she will interface with all levels of employees and management, including Board of Directors and senior management, to support our ELT members in the execution of a broad range of initiatives. They will coordinate and maintain schedules, travel, and any other tasks as well as project management, follow ups on meetings and board and other strategic meeting agendas, management and logistics. This position is based at the company’s corporate headquarters in New York. This person will also support the planning, organizing and managing of company events on-site and virtual.


Digital Product Designer



The Digital Product Designer is responsible for creating an enjoyable and intuitive user experience. With the input from the design and business perspective, the Digital Product Designer draws ideas to determine which is best suited for the product and communicates these ideas effectively to the team. At "In a Box", design is a core component in fulfilling our vision of sparking discovery, connection, and joy through cooking. For us, design is not just the way things look or feel, but also how they work. We have a complex product that needs to be simple and inviting for all customers. The Digital Consumer Design team is responsible for the entire end-to-end digital customer experience. We’re a lean, empathetic team of go-getters that work collaboratively with every team at In a Box. The products and experiences you design will impact every In a Box customer and the business.
