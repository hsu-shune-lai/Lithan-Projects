Glazed Pork Belly & Ramen Noodles

Instructions:

  1. Sauté earthy mushrooms and sweet corn with aromatic scallions and ginger.
  2. Toss them together with tender ramen noodles in an umami-rich miso, soy, and butter sauce.
  3. Top it all with sweet and spicy honey-gochujang glazed pork belly, crisp marinated cucumbers, and jammy soft-boiled eggs.

Cooking Time:

Approximately 50 minutes

Price:

$6.99 per serving (2 Servings)
